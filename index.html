Rock, Papper, Scissors is a simple game that involves two players. In this version you will play against the computer. Select one of three options: rock, papper, or scissors. The objective is to determine the winner based on the choices made. Rock defeats scissors, scissors defeats papper, and papper defeats rock. The players compare their choices, and the option that defeats the other is declared the winner. If both players make the same choice, it's a tie. It's a quick and entertaining way to settle decisions or have fun!
